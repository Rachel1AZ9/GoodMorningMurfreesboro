ABOUT &
THE TEAM

Built by locals, for locals — meet the people who wake up every morning to tell Murfreesboro's story.

Our Mission

TO INFORM, CELEBRATE, AND CONNECT THE MURFREESBORO COMMUNITY — ONE MORNING AT A TIME.

We believe every community deserves a platform that puts people first. Good Morning Murfreesboro exists to shine a light on the events, organizations, and individuals that make the Boro one of Tennessee's greatest places to live, work, and grow.

Our Story

WHY WE
DO THIS.

Good Morning Murfreesboro started with a simple idea: what if there was one place where Murfreesboro residents could start their day fully informed, genuinely inspired, and deeply connected to their community?

We saw non-profits doing incredible work that nobody knew about. We saw local businesses opening their doors without a single mention. We saw events come and go without enough people showing up. That had to change.

So we built Good Morning Murfreesboro — a daily community hub that puts local voices front and center, every single morning.
